Active Alignment

Sick of that reoccurring pain in your shoulder blade or low back? Frustrated that you can’t get up off the floor easily anymore? Deal with tension headaches on a regular basis? Shoulder or hip mobility limiting you from hitting a PR in that lift? Can’t turn your neck without it hurting?

Active Alignment was designed for you. The person that is ready to take charge of their life and say enough is enough. Pain and limited functional mobility do not need to limit you from living your best life & doing all the things you love to do. A comprehensive assessment and treatment approach; looking at the body as a whole & providing you with the tools to continue toward achieving your goals.

What to expect at your Active Alignment Evaluation:

  • Thorough review of your intake form. Open discussion of health & injury history, short & long-term goals, and current daily habits (activity, hydration, stress levels, sleep quality, nutrition, etc.).
  • Detailed static & dynamic/functional assessment of the body + specific orthopedic testing depending on your personal goals.
  • Bodywork! This can include any & all techniques I offer that you are comfortable with – PNMT, cup therapy/myofascial decompression, scraping, Myoskeletal Alignment Techniques, and more! With this work, and for the best results, you are expected to actively participate throughout the sessions.
  • Education & instruction on self-mobility techniques and corrective exercises to complete between sessions tailored specifically to your goals.
  • Collaborative discussion on findings from evaluation + what is a realistic continued frequency for future sessions.

What Active Alignment is not:

  • A diagnosis. All the evaluation and assessment that occurs is to help clue me into what muscles & structures of the body are involved and not performing as well as they could be. Doctors, Physical Therapists, or Chiropractors are the ones you need to see if something serious is going on & a diagnosis needs to occur. I can help with knowing when that referral might be needed.
  • A passive therapy. You are expected and required to participate in assessments & treatments. Multiple different positions may occur during a single session so wearing athletic clothing is required. Research shows quicker and longer lasting changes occur when you are actively engaged in your care.
  • A full body session. Although I am looking at the body as a whole and treating it as a whole, I will not work on every single area. Specific muscles or joint areas are addressed as needed based on the findings from assessments.
If you’ve made it this far, awesome! I would love to help you achieve your goals. Along with everything else I already mentioned you also get these things:
  • Open communication & guidance from me throughout the way via whatever method you prefer (call, text, email). Weekly check-ins + accountability to ensure the exercises and lifestyle changes are working well for you or what we can do to make things better.
  • Don't have a gym membership or exercise equipment at home? No worries. I will work with what you do have and even provide some bands if those are the best corrective exercises that we feel you need.
  • Upon completion of your evaluation, you will also receive an exclusive Align Bodywork massage ball so you have the tool to help with any soft tissue self-release work.
